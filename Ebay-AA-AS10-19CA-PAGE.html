10'' Diameter LED Barn Light Fixture With 19 3/4'' Curved Arm

Attentively designed and manufactured from solid aluminum,

HTM Lighting's Curved Arm Modern Barn Lights are painted with durable powder coating paint in order to enable superior protection, even in the toughest environments!

This beautiful redefined Modern Curved Arm Barn Light Fixture is suitable for indoor/outdoor use, and comes with an already attached swivel to enable you to control the light beam where it is needed!

The curved arm comes with a pre-wired hollow round mounting plate, in order to mount the fixture seamlessly onto any round standard junction box!

Through extended Research & Development, HTM Lighting has determined that a PAR30 or A19 LED Bulb would work best when using this fixture. For sign lighting, we recommend spacing each fixture about 3ft apart, in order to get a nice flow of light!

Great For Sign Lighting, Barn Lighting, Awnings, Restaurants, Storefronts, Wall Lighting and More!
